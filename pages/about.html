About us

Our passion to improve every aspect of the tobacco curing process sets us apart from many of our competitors.

ROC Systems is a tobacco curing solution supplier based in Zimbabwe. The primary product that we manufacture is the Ecotunnel - a novel tobacco curing system.

We make 5 different sizes, capable of handling 20, 28, 36, 42 & 48 Ha crops in a single planting. These units can be used to cure up to 2 further crops in a season when planted at monthly intervals.

Our technical team has designed a unique heat exchanger which operates on a dry heat system, thereby eliminating the necessity for a boiler unit and the high maintenance costs. The heating system has also been designed to reduce operating costs to the customer by considerable reductions in coal usage, diesel for generator power and electricity costs.

Our systems are designed to be highly versatile in application. Our industry-reach has the potential to extend well beyond tobacco. For example, the ROC Systems heating system could be adapted for commercial use in dryers for grain, coffee, onions and paprika.

Additionally, we manufacture roofing structures and supply roof sheets for sheds, as well as the manufacture of other steel structures as per our customers requirements.

Our vision is to become the most sought-after tobacco curing equipment suppliers in Southern Africa.


We would like to thank all our customers and employees that have supported us over the years. Looking forward to many more!
